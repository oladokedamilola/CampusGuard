Tips for Better Reports
Photo Tips
  • Take clear, well-lit photos
  • Capture multiple angles
  • Include landmarks for location
  • Show damage/evidence clearly
Description Tips
  • Be specific about what happened
  • Note exact time and date
  • Describe people involved (if safe)
  • Mention any witnesses
Safety First
  • Do not put yourself in danger
  • Blur faces in photos if needed
  • Report anonymously if concerned
  • Contact authorities for emergencies
